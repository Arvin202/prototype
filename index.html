About us

What are we doing?

We provide products that can effortlessly and environmentally deal with kitchen waste. We are also committed in promoting composting, biocomposting and vermicomposting.

What inspires us?

We were inspired by SDGS Goal 12: Responsible consumption and production, which aims to reduce waste through prevention, reduction, recycling and reuse. Moreover, according to Food Waste Index Report 2021 by UNEP, billions of tons food are wasted every year, which will generate substantial pollution, such as Dioxin (through burning) and CO2. According to studies, only 10% of kitchen waste was properly tackled and the proportion of kitchen waste in trash is over 40%. This will cause the excessive amount of water was burned and the incinerator will be vulnerable according. Therefore, we advocate individuals and households to decrease their waste on daily routine.
